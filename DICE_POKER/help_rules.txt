GAME RULES:
1. Dice Poker is turn based game where players roll their dices trying to collect certain figures and get points for completing them.
2. At the start of each turn after the dices are thrown and you're given a hand which contains your rolled dices.
3. After your rolls or re-rolls are finished you have to select figure that you want to get points for by typing its index number or cross it out by typing its name
4. The cycle is repeated until all players have finished their point table
