DICE FIGURES 

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
|	FIGURE		|		HOW TO OBTAIN?            |   		POINTS     		|
|-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|	
| Five of a Kind	| 5 dices of the same value               | Sum of your hand + 50 bonus points  |
|-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Four of a Kind        | 4 dices of the same value               | Sum of 4 dices + 20 bonus points    |
|-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Full House		| Combination of Three of a Kind and Pair | Sum of your hand + 10 bonus points  |
|-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Large Straight        | Dices from 2 to 6			  | 20 points                           |
|-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Small Straight        | Dices from 1 to 5                       | 15 points                           |
|-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Three of a Kind       | 3 dices of the same value               | Sum of 3 dices                      |
|-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Two Pairs             | Two times two dices with the same value | Sum of dices which are in pairs     |
|-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Pair                  | 2 dices of the same value               | Sum of a pair                       |
|-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| All Odd               | All dices in hand are odd               | Sum of your hand                    |
|-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| All Even              | All dices in hand are even              | Sum of your hand                    |
|-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Chance                | Any combination of figures in your hand | Sum of your hand                    |
